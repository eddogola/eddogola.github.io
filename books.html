A passionate lover of words, I am enchanted by literary works, particularly fiction, but I am trying to expand the domain of books I read. Currently, I am looking to read political and economic history, African literature, sci-fi, genetics, physics, and more classic fiction.

Mon, Apr 22, 2024: My current goal, however vain, is to be familiar with the classics, build an encyclopedic knowledge, and be able to call myself a ravenous, omnivorous reader; an aspiration, which as of now, is an island a far cry away. Ultimately, through the process, I want to truly think, and derive the pleasures and worthwhile sorrow that deep contemplation brings.

My best books of all time are The Collector by John Fowles, Deep Work by Cal Newport, East of Eden and Misery by Stephen King, both influencing my life, immensely, so far.
