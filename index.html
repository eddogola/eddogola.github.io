Hey! I am Ogola Eddy. I think of myself as an artist and scientist, existing in this creative intersection that lends me the privilege of chasing innovation while stamping my work with my own idiosyncratic creativity. I am currently a sophomore at MIT, pursuing a double major in Computer Science and Engineering (6-3) and Mathematics (18), and concentrating in writing (21W).

My technical interests lie in computer systems and artificial intelligence. I am still exploring both, very overlapping, fields and working towards finding a research area I am very curious about in either spaces. I enjoy poetry, reading, and taking photographs whenever I can.
